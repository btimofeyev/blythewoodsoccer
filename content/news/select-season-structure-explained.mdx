The Select calendar changes by age group, especially once players reach high school ages.

## U13 and U14

These teams may have opportunities in both fall and spring depending on roster interest and the right competitive setup. Players at this stage often have more flexibility to balance club play with school commitments.

## U15 and older

Older teams typically focus on the fall competitive season, since many players move into high school soccer each spring. At these ages, team performance, consistency, and competitive demands usually increase.

## League context

Blythewood Select teams compete in South Carolina Youth Soccer structures that use promotion and relegation. That gives the club a cleaner way to find the right competitive level for each team over time.
