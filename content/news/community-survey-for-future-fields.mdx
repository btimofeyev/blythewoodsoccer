Richland County Recreation Commission is shaping its long-range master plan, and public input matters.

For Blythewood families, this conversation is larger than a single field request. The region needs durable infrastructure that can keep up with growth and support multiple sports, safer scheduling, better weather resilience, and stronger family amenities.

## Key needs the club is highlighting

- Lighted soccer fields
- Lighted turf options that reduce cancellations
- Space for multiple age groups and sports at the same time
- Parking, restrooms, and spectator infrastructure that scale with community demand

The club is asking families to take the survey and clearly advocate for a full sports complex that can support long-term community growth.
