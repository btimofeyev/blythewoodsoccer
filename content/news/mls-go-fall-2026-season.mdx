Registration for the MLS GO Fall 2026 recreational season is open.

## Registration fees

- **Regular registration:** June 11, 2026 – August 10, 2026 · **$110**
- **Late registration:** August 10, 2026 – August 17, 2026 · **$130**

## Season dates

- **Practices and games begin:** Week of August 17, 2026
- **Last game of the season:** October 2, 2026

## Location

**Richland County Wellness Center**  
306 Flora Dr  
Columbia, SC 29223

## Practice and game schedule

- **Practices:** One-hour Coerver sessions focused on skills and technical ability
- **Practice days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday
- **Practice times:** 5 PM, 6 PM, or 7 PM
- **Games:** Friday nights

## MLS GO benefits

- Full uniform from an MLS team—shirt, shorts, and socks—included with every registration
- One ticket per child to a Charlotte FC home match

Register through PlayMetrics to secure a spot before the registration window closes.
