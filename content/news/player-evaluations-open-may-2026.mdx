Player evaluations are now open for the next Blythewood competitive cycle.

These sessions are for players who want to be considered for Blythewood competitive teams. Coaches use evaluations to assess current level, roster fit, and placement.

## What to know

- Evaluations help with placement but do not require any long-term commitment.
- Families will receive follow-up communication about roster fit.
- If the club cannot place a player immediately, staff will help point families toward the best next option.

## Why this matters

If a player is ready to explore the competitive side of the club, this evaluation window is the right place to start.

Use the PlayMetrics evaluation link to register.
